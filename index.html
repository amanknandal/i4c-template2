Highlights / Events

The Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (I4C) is an initiative by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, to tackle cybercrime comprehensively. Launched in 2018, I4C coordinates law enforcement efforts across the country and improves capabilities to handle cybercrime through modern technology and data analysis.

National Conference on Cyber Safety & National Security

The Union Minister for Home and Cooperation, Shri Amit Shah addressed the National Conference on Cyber Safety and National Security (Azadi from Cybercrime Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav) in New Delhi on 20th June 2022.

Launch of I4C Newsletter 'Cyber Pravaha'

The Union Home Secretary unveiled the first edition of I4C newsletter 'Cyber Pravaha'. Its an initiative to share precise information related to Cybercrime with Law Enforcement Agencies and other stakeholders.
